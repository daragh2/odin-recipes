Prawn Curry

Home

Description

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown. Add garlic and ginger, cooking until fragrant (1-2 minutes).
  2. Cook Tomatoes: Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until softened and saucy, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add Spices: Stir in curry powder, cumin, ground coriander, and chili powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes to release the flavors.
  4. Simmer Sauce: Pour in the coconut milk and mix well. Add water if needed to adjust consistency. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add Prawns: Toss the prawns into the sauce and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until they turn pink and are cooked through.
  6. Season and Garnish: Taste and adjust salt. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice, naan, or flatbread, along with a squeeze of lemon or lime.
